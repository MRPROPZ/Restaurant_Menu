#1 Tomyumkung (Best seller!!!)

A well-known Thai hot and sour soup, Tom Yum Kung is made of shrimp, lemongrass, galangal, kaffir lime leaves, lime juice, fish sauce, and chili. It is well known for having a strong, hot, and pungent flavor that symbolizes Thai cuisine. (Price: 150 Baht/4.34 USD)

#2 Padthai (Should Try!!!)

Prepared with rice noodles, eggs, shrimp or tofu, bean sprouts, and peanuts, pad Thai is one of the favorite Thai stir-fried noodle dishes that has a flavor out of lime, fish sauce, and tamarind sauce. Locals and tourists alike prefer it because it provides an ideal combination of savory, salty, sour, and sweet flavor.
(Price: 60 Baht/1.74 USD).

#3 Somtamthai (Spicy Meal!!!)

Spicy and sour, som tam (Thai papaya salad) is made up of shredded green papaya, fish sauce, lime juice, peanuts, garlic, and chili. The northeastern part of Thailand is renowned for its strong flavors and texture of this dish. (Price: 70 Baht/2.03 USD).
